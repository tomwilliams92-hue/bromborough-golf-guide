⚑ Your one blind spot

7-iron (162) → 6-iron (185) is a 23-yard gap. Anything needing 163–184 carry is between clubs — it bites on the 6th (173 par 3) and the 14th (~167 approach). Decide now: smooth 6 or hard 7, and stick to it.

Carry vs. total

These are carry numbers. On firm summer turf add roughly +15–25 yds roll off the tee. Into greens, carry is king — over every ravine and pond, club for the carry, not the number.

The course leans right

OB or a penalty sits right on holes 1, 3 (long), 4, 9 and 18. If your miss is a push or fade, that's the shot that wrecks the card — manage it.

The big picture

Game Plan

Slope 142 means Bromborough punishes mistakes more than it rewards heroics. Bank your score on the short holes, take the pars they give you on the brutes, and never flirt with the OB.
